# Daily Learning Journal

Disclaimer: These are just inspirations that have proven to be effective, feel free to add / skip / alter them.

## Morning

- Read your journal entry from yesterday evening.
- Try to remember the core concepts from yesterday. Draw out where they connect into the map of things you already know.
- Choose one thing from the last day plus one thing from the past that you want to review during the day.
- Set one thing you want to focus on doing particularly well today.
- What is the next larger learning goal that's set at the moment? What is the next step for today to reach that goal.
- Brain dump all things you need to do outside of class and store them separately, so you don't have to worry about them during class.

## Evening

- Read your journal entry from this morning. Did you follow through?
- What was easy and why?
- What was hard and why?
- What have you forgotten?
- One thing you are grateful for.
- One thing you did to help someone else today.
- What and how could you imporve tomorrow?

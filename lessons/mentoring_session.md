# Mentoring Session

## Student Introduction

**Fun Question:** What was the very first thing you did after finishing school?

## Mentor Introduction

**Fun Question:** What was the very first thing you programmed?

## Conversation Questions

- Who are you?
- What do you do?
- What do you want to do?
- Which new technologies do you want to learn?
- How do you learn? 
- What is your secret weapon when it comes to learning?
- Share something you build and why you like it.
- What was a big learning?
- What was a struggle as a developer that you've overcome?
